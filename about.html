About The Sports Shop

The Sports Shop was founded in 2020 and is based out of Sligo, Ireland.

We started as a college project by founder and CEO Luke Sweeney, and have since strived to deliver the best quality sports goods to our customers.


As we are a new company, we would love to hear your thoughts about us so we can improve our service and become the #1 sports shop in the country!
